About Me

Hello, my name is James Triveri.

I am a senior data scientist at Arity, a mobility data and analytics company focused on making transportation smarter, safer, and more useful for everyone. My primary interests are ML/DL, scientific computing and performance optimization.

I’m an ardent supporter of Free Software, as well as a long-time user and advocate of the Python programming language. I am an active contributor to Open Source, and maintain a few projects of my own. You can check out my GitHub page here.

I graduated from Augustana College in Rock Island, Illinois with training in Mathematics and Physics and received an M.S. in Computer Science from the Georgia Institute of Technology specializing in Machine Learning.

My favorite animal is the sloth (the Bradypodidae variety).

My CV is available here.

Thanks for checking in!
